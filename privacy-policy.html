1. Overview

Platfer is a learning platform used by students and teachers for digital learning activities such as classes, content sharing, and tests.

2. Authentication (Google OAuth)

Platfer may allow you to sign in with Google using OAuth. When you use Google sign-in, we receive basic account information from Google (for example, your name, email address, and a Google account identifier) as required to create and secure your Platfer account.

3. Services Used (Firebase)

Platfer uses Firebase services, which may include:

  • Firebase Authentication (sign-in and account security)
  • Cloud Firestore (storing app data related to your account and usage)

4. Data We Collect

Depending on how you use Platfer, we may collect:

  • Account details (name, email, phone number or identifiers)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • App usage data necessary to provide features
  • Device and technical information for reliability and security

5. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate users and keep accounts secure
  • Provide core app features for students and teachers
  • Maintain and improve app reliability and prevent abuse

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers used to operate the app (such as Firebase) or when required by law.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@platfer.app

By using Platfer, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
